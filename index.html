Our Story

Christian worked overnight as a tech lead to support agents. One fateful night, December 22, 2014, a beautiful new woman in the office grabbed Christian's attention immediately. As he got to know Chimére, Christian realized that she was not only cute but smart, quirky and very loving. After she invited him to go to the Dominican Republic with a group of her friends, Christian decided to befriend Chimére and hang out with her every weekend; such has been the case since her birthday celebration in 2015.

In fact, since then they've fallen in love and created spectacular memories together. Their magical adventures include attending beer festivals, quinching culinary cravings & bike pedaling in Seattle, pub hopping in Atlanta's L5P, hookah lounging in San Francisco, roller coaster riding in Orlando, slot machine exploration in Las Vegas and truly the list grows with each month.

They are life travel partners. They are lovers. They are very best friends.

How he Proposed

“I decided to make a career shift into the tech business. I was pretty stoked about my new position at this youthful, quirky company, which aligned well with my personality. So after weeks of training, swapped my entire lifestyle to third shift and went into this new environment. I considered myself pretty tech savvy already but the learning curve got super real and I had plenty of questions to make sure I was on top of things.

“Christian worked the same shift and when he wasn’t shooting me with NERF gun ammunition, he was assisting agents with technical question they came across. I noticed that every question that stumped me was easily answered by Christian. I even tried asking him super random doozies, like ‘Let’s see if he knows this!’ And he knew (or he’d know where to find the info)! “In a short period, I grew to know him as the ‘smartest guy in the room’ with leadership qualities that I respected. I invited him on a trip to the Dominican Republic with some other friends of mine, and so we started hanging out on weekends. Our first cool non-work moment was when we decided to show each other our childhood homes on Google Maps! Complete nerds, lol.

“Initially, I just approached ours as a nice friendship since he was younger than I and everyone knows that dating at work can get messy. A couple of months into our friendship, though, it suddenly occurred to me that Christian was courting me with such care and respect that I’d always wanted from my future husband. We shared so many laughs and experiences as friends, so it was a no-brainer when I fell for him at Front Page News, where we were grabbing a bite before our evening shift started. “Today, he still gazes at me like he did when we first met and calls me ‘Beautiful’ like it’s scripted on my forehead. I pray that never changes and I know I’m blessed beyond measure to become his wife.”
